The Company processes personal information for the following purposes, and the Company will notify Users in advance and obtain their consent if it intends to use personal information beyond the scope of the following purposes.

1. User Registration and Management

  • User Identification
  • Prevention of Fraudulent Use of Services
  • Sending Notifications

2. Provision and Operation of Services

  • Verification of Service and Content Eligibility
  • Processing Payments
  • Provision of Service Features and Contents
  • Analysis of User Activities

3. Development and Improvement of Services

  • Development of New Services
  • Improvement of Existing Service Features and Contents

4. Resolution of Customer Grievances

  • Reception of Inquiries
  • Provision of Customer Services
  • Keeping Records for Resolution of Legal and Contractual Issues

III. Data Retention Policy

The Company processes and retains personal information for a period specified by its internal policies. However, some information will be processed and retained by the period specified by relevant laws for compliance.

[Retention Period According to Internal Policies]

  • Information Collected During the Use of Mobile Apps: Until the withdrawal of membership
  • Information Collected During the Use of VR Apps in Meta Quest Devices: Until the deletion of the Meta account used in the VR Application

[Retention Period According to Relevant Laws]

1. ACT ON THE CONSUMER PROTECTION IN ELECTRONIC COMMERCE, ETC

  • Records related to indications and advertisements (6 months)
  • Records related to resolution of consumer complaints or disputes (3 years)
  • Records related to cancellation of contracts or subscriptions, etc. (5 years)
  • Records related to payments and supply of goods, etc. (5 years)

2. PROTECTION OF COMMUNICATIONS SECRETS ACT

  • Website Access Logs (3 months)

VI. Installation, Operation, and Rejection of Devices that Automatically Collect Personal Information

To provide individualized services and convenience to users, the Company uses cookies which store and retrieve information frequently.

Cookies are small pieces of information sent to the browser of a User by the server (http) operating the website. Cookies are stored on the PC or mobile device of a User.

Users can configure their web browser options to allow or block cookies. However, refusing the use of cookies may cause difficulties when using personalized services.

[How to Allow/Block Cookies on a Web Browser]

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Delete browsing data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data

[How to Allow/Block Cookies on a Mobile Browser]

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Delete browsing data
  • Safari: Settings (on device) > Safari > Advanced > Block All Cookies
  • Samsung Internet: Settings > Personal browsing data > Delete browsing data
